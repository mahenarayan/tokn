# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and professional collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss ideas and code on their technical merits
- be precise, constructive, and willing to revise
- assume good intent until there is evidence otherwise
- respect different levels of experience and backgrounds

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- personal attacks
- deliberate misrepresentation
- doxxing or publishing private information
- disruptive conduct that prevents productive collaboration

## Scope

This applies to project spaces and public interactions representing the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `maheshn1@icloud.com`.
Include enough detail for the report to be actionable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or ban participants when required to protect the project and its contributors.
